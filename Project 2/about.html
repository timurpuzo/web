Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About La Bascarsija

Since its inception in 1920, our café nestled in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina, has been a beacon of tradition. Founded by the esteemed Hernandez family, our commitment to excellence runs deep. We meticulously curate our coffee beans, sourcing only the finest from South and Central America, ensuring each cup embodies the essence of our heritage.

Step into our café and be transported to a world where every sip tells a story—a story of dedication, craftsmanship, and a profound love for coffee. Here, amidst the comforting aroma of tradition, you'll experience firsthand the artistry that goes into each cup. Join us in celebrating Sarajevo's rich coffee culture, where every moment is an invitation to savor the finer things in life.
